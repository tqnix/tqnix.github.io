Welcome to the Button Brothers(CO) ButtonSuite!

The ButtonSuite is an app made to generate button designs more easily, with a preview showing you how your button will look like.

Controls:
Tap/Click: Move circle in Edit Mode
Hold: Drag circle in Edit Mode
-/+ | Q/E: Adjust selection circle diameter
WASD: Precision Movement
Z (press twice): Undo
1,2,3,4,5: Edit, Preview, Settings, Help, and Full Image View tabs respectively
8,9,0: Import, Export, and Center (selection circle) respectively

Getting Started:
Press the "Import" button to open the import dialog, and import an image.
If some elements are cut off when you're in Edit or Preview Mode, or you only see 1 green bar onscreen, then zoom out, resize your browser window, or change your device or monitor to portrait/vertical mode.
The current mode you are in and the circle size and position are displayed at the bottom right.
To change the mode/tab you are in, click one of the buttons at the top left.

Using the ButtonSuite:
Move the circle with your cursor and adjust the size using the buttons on the top right, and go to Preview Mode to see what the button will look like.
You can back to Edit Mode to edit the selection circle placement with the full image again.
You can also use WASD or the buttons on the bottom right to finely adjust the circle position by 1 image pixel, or press the "0" button onscreen to move the selection circle to the center of the screen.
It's also possible to undo circle movement, precision circle movement, changing the background or selection circle color, and circle size change with Z.

Once you're happy with how your button looks, click/tap "Export" to export your button as a photo. Note that any whitespace/transparency will be replaced by the background color.
(Afterwards, please make sure to put it in the order form, or email us through our business email: buttonbros.ldhss@gmail.com)
If you would like the change the background or selection circle color, go to the Settings tab, where you can find a bunch of other customization options, such as turning on/off the crosshair, or changing the selection circle's color.
